Bivariate Analysis



Interactive scatterplots rendering two US Census variables on X and Y axis; joined on US county.

County color scheme based on 5 US region for comparative regional filtering.

Hover over dot elements for individual county data points.
Hover over region name to animate filtering of regional data points.
Hover over state name for animation filtering of county.
Click on state name to filter data for inspection of individual state data points.

Data Source: 2013 American Community Survery (ACS) - 3 Year Summary File

Choropleths



Interactive maps rendering unique US Census variables for all US counties ; color gradiation based on particular variable value

Hover over individual county for county name and variable value.
Click on individual county to recolor US map to filter counties with higher values relative to selection
Click on grey box in top right color to toggle between counties with higher and lower values relative to county clicked.
To Zoom in and out of map: Place two digits on keypad and bring both digits up to zoom in. Bring both digits down to zoom out.

Data Source: 2013 American Community Survery (ACS) - 3 Year Summary File

Stop and Frisk : NYC 2014



Map of Stop and Frisk incidents by geolocational coordinates

45,000 incidents rendered as goelocational dots over map of NYC zip codes.

Color scheme based on racial categories as established by the NYPD.

Click on blue sidebars to explore stops which yielded contraband or assault weapons.
To Zoom in and out of map: Place two digits on keypad and bring both digits up to zoom in. Bring both digits down to zoom out.

Data Source: Thank you to BetaNYC
http://data.beta.nyc/dataset/stop-question-and-frisk-data
